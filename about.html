About & Contact

I work to combine art, design, and technology to create digital experiences and experiments that mix these disciplines and others in novel and interestingly different ways. I build websites, interactive exhibitions, and experimental code, I take photographs, I create photorealistic architectural and environmental renderings. Much of this work can be found in my portfolio.


Additionally, I build a variety of physical and digital things to learn about new processes. I write and occasionally draw too. See some of these assorted random things in the more frequently updated projects section.


For the most part, I am self-taught, and have been using tools such as C++, Java, Unreal Engine 4, Photoshop, and 3Ds Max to create a wide array of different work. I constantly am looking for new mediums and processes to explore that merge art, design, and technology. Currently I'm a student at Carnegie Mellon University in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. For inquiries, feel free to contact me by email.
