PROCESS:

I make a variety of types of architectural renderings and visualizations. Most begin with a Revit model provided by the client. I apply materials and optimize the lighting in the scene and create a rendering. From there, I finish the view in Photoshop with additional foliage, materials, people, and other details. How much work is done in Photoshop depends on the level of detail in the provided Revit model.

Alternatively, I can make simpler renderings only in Photoshop for less finalized models. These are usually elevations or exterior views, colored with materials.

Finally, I can also work in Unreal Engine 4 to create realtime walkthroughs and videos of architecture. These projects are often photorealistic and highly detailed. Contact me for more information or a quote.
